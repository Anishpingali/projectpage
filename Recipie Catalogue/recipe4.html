Vegetable Spring Rolls

Ingredients:

  • Spring roll wrappers
  • 1 cup cabbage, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup carrots, julienne
  • 1/2 cup bell peppers, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup bean sprouts
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, minced
  • 1 teaspoon garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a pan. Saut√© ginger and garlic until fragrant.
  2. Add cabbage, carrots, bell peppers, and bean sprouts. Cook until vegetables are slightly tender.
  3. Drizzle soy sauce over the vegetables and season with salt and pepper. Mix well.
  4. Place a spring roll wrapper on a clean surface. Add a spoonful of the vegetable mixture in the center.
  5. Fold the sides of the wrapper over the filling, then roll tightly from the bottom.
  6. Seal the edge with a bit of water.
  7. Repeat with the remaining wrappers and filling.
  8. Heat oil in a pan for frying. Fry the spring rolls until golden brown and crispy.
  9. Drain excess oil on a paper towel.
  10. Serve hot with dipping sauce of your choice.
