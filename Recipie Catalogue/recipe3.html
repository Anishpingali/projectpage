Paneer Butter Masala

Ingredients:

  • 200g paneer (cottage cheese), cubed
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 teaspoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Directions:

  1. In a pan, melt butter and sauté the chopped onions until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute.
  3. Add tomato puree, garam masala, turmeric powder, cumin powder, chili powder, and salt. Cook until the mixture thickens and the oil separates.
  4. Stir in heavy cream and cook for a few minutes.
  5. Add paneer cubes and simmer for another 5-7 minutes.
  6. Garnish with fresh cilantro.
  7. Serve hot with naan or rice.
