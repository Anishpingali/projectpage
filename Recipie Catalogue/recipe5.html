Chicken Manchuria

Ingredients:

  • 500g boneless chicken, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon chili sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Oil for frying
  • For the sauce:
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 bell pepper, finely chopped
  • 3-4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon tomato ketchup
  • 1 teaspoon chili sauce
  • 1 teaspoon vinegar
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • Salt to taste

Directions:

  1. In a bowl, mix chicken pieces with all-purpose flour, cornstarch, ginger-garlic paste, soy sauce, chili sauce, black pepper, and salt. Let it marinate for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil for frying in a pan. Fry the marinated chicken until golden brown and crispy. Drain on paper towels.
  3. In a separate pan, heat a little oil. Saut√© chopped onion, bell pepper, and minced garlic until fragrant.
  4. Add soy sauce, tomato ketchup, chili sauce, vinegar, sugar, and salt. Mix well to create the sauce.
  5. Add the fried chicken to the sauce and toss until coated.
  6. Garnish with chopped spring onions and serve hot.
