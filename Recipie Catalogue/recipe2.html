Butter Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 500g boneless chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 teaspoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Directions:

  1. Marinate the chicken in yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric powder, cumin powder, chili powder, and salt for at least 1 hour.
  2. In a pan, melt butter and saut√© the chopped onions until golden brown.
  3. Add the marinated chicken and cook until it's no longer pink.
  4. Stir in garam masala and tomato puree. Cook for a few minutes.
  5. Pour in the heavy cream and simmer for another 5-7 minutes.
  6. Garnish with fresh cilantro.
  7. Serve hot with naan or rice.
